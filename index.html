Hi,
My name is Michal Chesny, a graphic designer. Graduat at Kibutzim College of Art & Education, Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.), Design and Applied Arts. Also I studied at Mentor- College of Design and New Media. The connection between aesthetics, clean lines and delicate shapes has become the guideline for my work, my designs, and my way of life. The inspiration for my designs is usually drawn from the smallest details, those that seem unimportant, for me they are the whole world.
